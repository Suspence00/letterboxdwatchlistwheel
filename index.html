Letterboxd Watchlist Wheel

Upload your exported watchlist, curate the entries, and spin to decide what to watch next.

1. Bring in your Letterboxd picks

Import directly from Letterboxd

Paste a Letterboxd list URL (like https://letterboxd.com/suspence0/list/the-wheel/) and we'll pull the entries right into the wheel.

Prefer to upload a CSV?

Export your watchlist from Letterboxd (Profile → Settings → Data → Export Your Data) and upload the watchlist.csv file here.

Need a CSV? Use the download helper to generate one from a list.
